Description:-

172 Kmph

17 km/l combined

Noise:-

The engine noise is high
and I Diesel engines make
a lot of noise while burning the
fuel. The main reason behind this is
that diesel molecules are much bigger
than petrol molecules and the engines

prominent reason behind the excessive
noise in diesel engines is the fact that
diesel engines do not use spark ignition.
